Tumusiime Charity

Humanitarian Response Specialist

Charity Tumusiime is a Monitoring and evaluation practitioner with special focus on community, health, gender and rural development initiatives in Uganda. Charity possesses a Masters’ degree and a Post Graduate Diploma in Monitoring &Evaluation and a B.A in development studies.

I have technical knowledge in gender and rural development disciplines, knowledge and skills in data entry, data quality assurance, analysis and report writing. She has more than five years’ experience in Humanitarian response and programme in refugees’ communities. Four of those years were with Oxfam as Monitoring Evaluation Accountability & Learning (MEAL) Assistant at Kyaka II Refugee Settlement. She also worked with World Vision Uganda at Adjumani Refugee Settlements taking lead in conducting all program assessments and price monitoring to assess the program outcomes and provide actionable recommendations to improve the program.
