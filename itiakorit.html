Harriet Itiakorit

Qualitative Researcher

Ms. Itiakorit is our qualitative research specialist. She has more than eight years’ experience on large scale biomedical trials and qualitative studies. Rising through ranks as a junior research assistant to a research coordinator overseeing the work of a social sciences team, Harriet has excellent written and verbal communication skills, supervision and mentoring other qualitative researchers. Her wide experience in management, data collection, coding and analyses of qualitative data is indispensable for the team.

Ms. Itiakorit has co-authored one published paper and has three co-authored papers in progress. She is the second author on one of these papers. Her first-authored abstract titled: “PrEP has kept me HIV-negative”: PrEP experiences among serodifferent couples in rural Kenya and Uganda during population-wide PrEP Implementation” was been accepted and presented at ICASA 2019 in Kigali, Rwanda.
