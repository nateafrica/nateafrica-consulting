Omiel Patrick

Management Information Systems Specialist

Patrick holds an MSc in Information Systems, Makerere University, Kampala; Post Graduate Diploma in Monitoring and Evaluation, Uganda Management Institute; and BSc in Computer Science & Mathematics, Makerere University, Kampala. Patrick has over 15 years of professional work experience and supports the team on adhoc basis.

At the moment, Patrick is a Senior Consultant in HISP Uganda (University of Oslo). His job entails the following: providing technical assistance to the Ministry of Health, Uganda through the National DHIS2 Core support team and eHealth Technical Working Group and providing technical support in the development of global WHO DHIS2 metadata packages for aggregate data capture and analysis. He also provides technical assistance for adoption and implementation of WHO Health Packages for program areas including – TB, HIV, Immunization, RMNCAH, Surveillance and Malaria in four (4) countries; Uganda, Zambia, Madagascar, and Malawi; and provides technical support for implementation of PEPFAR Global reporting system (DATIM); trainings, system maintenance, and user support and continuous system development. He is also the technical Lead in customizing DHIS2 for pilot in Education Management Information systems (EMIS) in Uganda; technical lead for short-term DHIS2 implementation projects for HISP Uganda; and provides support training at regional DHIS2 Academies in the Eastern and Southern Africa.

From 2015–2018, Patrick was the Health Information Systems Analyst at The US Centres for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC Uganda). While in this position, Patrick provided technical assistance and support in planning, designing, reviewing, and implementation of new and improved health information systems for CDC HIV/AIDS projects and implementing civil society partners and the Ministry of Health, Uganda. From 2011 to 2015, Patrick was a Data Management Specialist, The US Centres for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC Uganda). His main responsibility was to develop and implement data management systems and data Quality Assurance. Patrick will lead the technical review processes and assessment of Device Manager and SMS reporting system and support the lead Auditor.
