Services

Our Services

NATE Africa has specialized in Planning, Monitoring, Evaluation, and Learning (MEL) alongside our strong culture of innovation and technology, to provide cutting edge, participatory, and right-sized MEL solutions for our clients in Africa

Our Services

NATE Africa works collaboratively for and in partnership with various organizations to provide technical expertise in the following key services areas;

Monitoring and Evaluation:

M&E system developments, Situational analysis, Needs analysis, Project baseline studies, Programme/Project Process, Outcomes and Impact evaluations, Diagnostic evaluation and Socio-Economic Profiles and Studies. Data collection and data quality assessments.

Planning and Project design:

Project conceptualization, Detailed project design, Plans of action, Strategic planning

Capacity building:

Organizes and delivers training to several organizations in the areas of planning, monitoring and Evaluation.

Data management:

Support data collection, collation, manipulation, analysis and presentation for utilization and evidence-based decisions
